Features

Every 6 days, the Survival Night begins. Prepare your defenses or face the horde.

üåô

Survival Night Events

Every 6 days (~2 hours real time), massive hordes spawn in waves every 30 seconds, hunting players until dawn. Survive to increase your gamestage.

üßüüíÄüï∑Ô∏è

22 Custom Mob Variants

10 zombies, 6 skeletons, 6 spiders. Each with unique abilities, roles, and AI. Tanks, chasers, ranged, support, DOT, and mini-bosses.

üìà

Gamestage Progression

Difficulty scales with days survived, kills, and survival nights completed. Higher gamestage unlocks deadlier zombie variants.

üè∞

Fortification Blocks

Spike traps, barbed wire, electric fences, and reinforced walls. All blocks have durability and show visual damage cracks.

üî®

Block Durability & Repair

All player-placed blocks have durability. Zombies damage blocks based on material. Repair with tiered hammers before they break!

üëÇ

Zombie Sensory System

Zombies detect sounds (block breaking, doors, combat) and can smell players during Survival Night. Villages are fallback targets.
